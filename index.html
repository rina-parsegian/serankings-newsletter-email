Protect Your Content From Google's AI
Overviews: Your Options

Google has tried hard to make AI Overviews accurate and useful, but some people aren't thrilled with the results. Many are even looking for ways to avoid it.

Here are a few options to limit or remove your content from appearing in these AI-generated responses:

•   Use preview control directives (like data-nosnippet or nosnippet HTML attributes) to control what appears in search snippets.

•   Disallow Googlebot from crawling (and indexing) your site or specific folders.

•   Use the noindex tag to remove pages from search results entirely; or use the meta tag or X-Robots to close specific pages from indexing.

•   To prevent AI Overviews from appearing in your search results, use the “Web” filter or try browser extensions like Hide AI Overviews, Bye Bye, and Google AI which is already available on the Chrome Web Store (note that this only hides them and doesn't stop Google from using your content).

Our take: No solution is perfect. Treat each of them with great caution. Any methods used to control how your website appears in Google's AI Overviews will affect its performance in traditional organic search results. There is currently no option to exclude your site specifically from AI Overviews. Also, it is unlikely that Google will introduce this capability.
